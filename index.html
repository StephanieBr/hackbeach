Who are we?

Just a bunch of people wanting to know more about the world (and some trying hard to fix the bugs they find).

What do we do?

Hacking

Putting our own spin on what already exists.

Art

Creating beauty with tools and technology.

Making

Creating physical things from scratch.

Teaching

Conduct workshops for locals, kids and students

Social

Connecting and sharing ideas and culture

How can you be a part of it?


There are many ways to be a part of Hackbeach. You could...

help to organise

Share your skills to make hackbeach happen: plan, negotiate, write, budget...

provide a
talk

Lecture an audience on your expertise in hacking, making, or art.

lead a workshop

Teach a small group hands-on. Software, carpentry, soldering, stretching...

spread the word!

Tell friends and colleagues or get hackbeach in the media.
